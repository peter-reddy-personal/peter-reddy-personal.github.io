Zwift Ladder route picker

What this tool does

The Zwift Ladder Route Picker helps choose the best race route based on the strengths of your team and the likely riders who will be racing with you. Enter your CLS riders and your expected opponents, set how likely each rider is to race, and the tool uses the input vELO2 scores to analyse every Zwift route to show where your team has the biggest advantage.

To input vELO2 scores you can type them in manually, or go to a rider's profile on ZwiftRacing.app, and copy-paste their most recent vELO2 scores (paste into the SPR box and they should fill across).

What does Lik% mean?

Likelihood (%) is your estimate of how likely each rider is to race. A rider with 100% is almost certain to be there, while a rider with 0% is very unlikely.

The Route Picker uses these percentages to weight each team’s strengths. Riders who are more likely to race have a bigger influence on the route recommendations. You can make up realistic values based on your own guesses, or set each rider to 100% (or any non-zero %) to not think about it.
